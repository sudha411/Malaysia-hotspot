Culture of Malaysia

Malaysia's culture is a beautiful blend of Malay, Chinese, Indian, and indigenous traditions. Scroll down to explore some highlights!

Traditional Dance

Malaysia's traditional dances like Zapin and Joget showcase the country’s rich artistic heritage and are performed during cultural events.

Traditional Dress

People wear Baju Melayu, Baju Kurung, Cheongsam, and Saree, reflecting the country's ethnic diversity during celebrations and formal events.

Arts and Crafts

Malaysia is known for its beautiful batik fabrics, songket weaving, and wood carving, which represent deep cultural significance.
