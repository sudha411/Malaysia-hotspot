Festivals of Malaysia

Malaysia celebrates many colorful festivals showcasing its multicultural heritage. Scroll down to explore some of the major ones!

Hari Raya Aidilfitri

The festival marking the end of Ramadan, celebrated with open houses, traditional dishes, and visiting family and friends.

Chinese New Year

Celebrated with lion dances, red lanterns, fireworks, and family gatherings to welcome the Lunar New Year.

Water Festival

Water Festival in Malaysia is just like Holi in India, except that they play it with clean uncoloured water and do not use dry colours as well

Thaipusam

A vibrant Hindu festival known for its procession to Batu Caves and acts of devotion and penance.
